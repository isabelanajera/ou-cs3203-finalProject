Hideaway Pizza

Hideaway easily has some of the best pizza on campus. Customize your own pizza with ease, by choosing from the wide variety of toppings they have available.


Canes

When it comes to fried chicken, Canes does it best! Canes offers many combos for your chicken needs.


Fuzzy's Taco Shop

Can't go wrong with classic tacos! Unlike other restaurants that serve tacos, Fuzzy's offers some of the craziest combinations.


Pho House

Feeling a little cold? Pho House offers Vietnamese style Asian cuisine. Grab some pho on a cold day, to warm you right up.


Pickleman's Gourmet Cafe

A long-standing campus favorite, Pickleman's offers sandwiches, salads, and so much more.


Tea Cafe

Tea cafe offers a nice, quiet environment where you can relax and hangout with some friends. They also offer boba teas and Asian-fusion dishes.


Louie's Grill and Bar

Louie's burgers are great! You can even watch your favorite sports while enjoying a meal.

Pink Berry

Frozen yogurt is always amazing on hot days. Who doesn't love fro-yo with all those toppings?
